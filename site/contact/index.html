Common Questions Before You Reach Out

What is your typical project budget?

Most engagements start at 25,000 EUR for focused brand projects and scale upward depending on scope. Product design and development projects typically range from 50,000 to 200,000 EUR. We scope every project individually and will be transparent about cost from the first conversation.

How long does a typical project take?

Brand identity projects run 8 to 12 weeks. Product design and development ranges from 3 to 6 months. We do not rush strategy. Timelines are set during discovery and held to rigorously.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

Both. We care about ambition, not company size. Some of our strongest work has come from early-stage founders who refused to launch with a generic identity. If you are serious about standing out, we want to hear from you.

Can we start with a small engagement first?

Yes. We offer a focused strategy sprint that runs two weeks and delivers positioning, competitive analysis, and a creative brief. It is designed as a standalone deliverable, but most clients continue into full execution.
