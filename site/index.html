Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of clients do you work with?

We work with ambitious brands that are ready to break conventions. Startups, scale-ups, and established companies that want work people remember. If you want safe and predictable, we are probably not the right fit. If you want bold, strategic, and unforgettable, get in touch.

How long does a typical project take?

Brand identity projects run six to ten weeks. Website design and development runs eight to fourteen weeks. Motion identity and video projects vary by scope. We scope every project individually because no two brands face the same challenge.

Do you work with clients outside your city?

Yes. Most of our clients are remote. We have delivered projects for brands in twelve countries across four continents. Time zones are not a barrier when the process is clear and the communication is direct.

What does it cost to work with BRUTAL.?

Our projects start at EUR 15,000 for brand strategy work and EUR 25,000 for full website builds. We price based on scope, not hours, because we believe in outcomes over timesheets. Get in touch and we will give you an honest answer fast.

Ready to Create Something Bold?

We are always looking for ambitious brands and fearless projects. No matter where you are in the world, if you have a challenge worth solving, we want to hear about it.
